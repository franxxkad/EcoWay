Plastic
You can recycle food cans, beverage cans. Before you hand over the cans for recycling, shake out all the food or liquid from them and rinse them to remove the residues.

Paper
Most cardboard mail boxes and food packages can be recycled. Many paper items such as newspapers, mail, and printer paper are also good.

Glass
Always empty and rinse glass containers before sending them for recycling. Do not throw away broken glass for recycling!

Metal
Some types of batteries, such as car batteries or lithium-ion batteries, are considered hazardous waste.
